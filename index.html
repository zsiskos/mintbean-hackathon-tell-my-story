About

This chart chronologically displays different milestones in my life and compares them based three variables:

  1. Age: when did it occur in my life
  2. Impact: on a scale of 1-5, how much did it affect me?
  3. Enjoyment: on a scale of 1-5, how happy did it make me?

The Redesign

The original site was created on for a hackathon. We were given the task and had four hours to create someting from scratch.

After a bit of reflection (and some YouTube tutorials) I created this redesign and in the same amount of time to implement some of new styles and features .
